A Look Inside...Pivotal

4 OCT 2017

404 is about discovering and discussing the unique tech companies in Ireland. The next company in our featured series is Pivotal and the evolution in their technology. 

Imagine having an idea in the morning and shipping before the day's over. It's closer than you think. But it requires a new approach to software. The payoff? Radical improvement in operational efficiency and developer productivity, reduced operating costs, enabling you to deliver better products faster than ever. Pivotal's Spring and Cloud Foundry, combined with our unique approach to agile development and continuous delivery at Pivotal Labs, are transforming how the world builds software.


Pivotal’s technology enable the leading companies in the world to transform with a modern approach focused on building—not buying—software. The team overcomes challenges associated with lengthy enterprise IT cycles by accelerating application delivery. Pivotal develops better products, together, using a proven methodology and the most powerful continuous innovation platform.

Their cloud-native platform is optimised for change so enterprises can move at start-up speeds and with greater business agility. Pivotal’s methodology is about evolving, in both development and innovation, and their culture is empowering. The team uses agile and lean approaches to teach next-generation developers to create and build new solutions. 

“We are very fortunate in Dublin to have a dedicated floor in the Dogpatch Labs co-working space, and we've benefited greatly from the thriving startup and industry ecosystem in Dublin.” 

In Dublin Pivotal’s product team is working on leading open source projects, including the Spring Java Framework, Cloud Foundry, and our new lifecycle management tool for Kubernetes, called Kubo, built in collaboration with Google and VMware. They have worked with high street banks, global automotive companies and financial giants to the Pivotal Labs team in Dublin to help transform their software development practices.
