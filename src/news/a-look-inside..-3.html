A Look Inside...Qualtrics

4 OCT 2017

With 404 only a few days away, we wanted to take a moment to explore Ireland’s unique tech companies. The next company to unveil is Qualtrics and what makes QLife the ideal working environment.  

Qualtrics helps people capture the data they need to do their jobs better. It’s a simple mission, powered by innovative technology and the 2,000+ Qualtrics staff that have an impact on making a product the customers love even better.

“Some might say we’re a hyper-growth, pre-IPO company on a rocket ship trajectory. But we’d rather just call it an opportunity”

Headquartered in Provo, Utah and listed at #6 in Forbes’ Cloud 100 listing in 2017, they have offices in every corner of the globe, including their EMEA HQ in Dublin, the heart of Europe’s tech scene.

Qualtrics’s Experience Management (XM) Platform powers 8,500 (and counting!) of the world’s biggest brands. It’s a market opportunity worth $15bn and they’re blazing a trail.

It’s helping organisations deliver industry-leading customer experience, develop must-have products, create amazing work environments and shape iconic brands.

 

QLife – Our Culture

“Our people turn great tech into a great tech company.”

The teams at Qualtrics give their all to make it a fantastic place to work, whether it’s the office perks - their very own Irish pub, massages, free food and drink and lots more - or the clubs and societies that make sure there’s plenty of downtime.

It’s all part of giving their teams what they need to pull together and drive the company forward.

Qualtrics works for a fantastic work environment while staying true to their core values, what they call TACOS:

  • Transparent - we’re a meritocracy and hold ourselves accountable. We reward the competent over the confident
  • All-in - we bet on Qualtrics and Qualtrics bets on us. This is our company. We deliver what it takes
  • Customer obsessed - if a customer is upset, we failed. Period. We learn and we fix it
  • One team - there is only one team at Qualtrics. We hunt as a pack. We win and lose together and never say, "That's not my job."
  • Scrappy - we're smart, resourceful, and find a way. We write our own story instead of following others

 

The Engineering team

Qualtrics credits their existence to the help of their engineers. Solutions architects, software development engineers, web developers and many more make the product what it is today.

And they never stop. Whether it’s a new feature at the request of a customer or an entirely new product to add to the stable, the teams feels successful if they continue to innovate and move Qualtric’s technology on.

That’s where you come in.

Join the seasoned full-stack engineers with experience in languages such as js, python and ruby and help Qualtric’s customers find new ways to use technology to drive key improvements in their customer, employee, brand and product experiences.
